Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application PokeValue Scanner for mobile devices.

1. User Authentication and Data Collection

Our application uses Firebase Authentication to allow users to create secure accounts. We collect and store your email address solely to manage your profile and save your personal trading card portfolio. We do not sell or share this information with third parties.

2. Camera Access and Usage

PokeValue Scanner requires access to your mobile device's camera. This permission is strictly used to capture real-time images of your physical trading cards for image recognition (AI analysis). The captured images are processed strictly to identify the card and calculate its value, and are not stored permanently on any remote server for promotional purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party service providers to power our application functionalities:

  • Firebase: For cloud database management and user secure sign-in.
  • RevenueCat: For managing and validating your Pro in-app subscription plans securely.
  • Pokémon TCG API: To retrieve official card data and live European market pricing (Cardmarket index).

4. Data Deletion

In compliance with Apple and Google strict guidelines, you can request the permanent deletion of your account and all your stored cards at any time directly through the app settings or by contacting our support email.
